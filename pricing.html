FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What's included in the implementation fee? ▾
Complete setup of your Soldiro instance, including API connections to Clay, Apollo, Hunter, and your CRM. Campaign configuration, team training, and 5 days of hands-on deployment support. You'll be live in under a week. Enterprises get a dedicated implementation manager.
Can I upgrade plans later? ▾
Absolutely. Upgrade at any time and we'll prorate the difference and migrate your data, campaigns, and settings seamlessly. Most teams upgrade from Core to Pro within 60 days once they see the results.
What's your cancellation policy? ▾
30-day money-back guarantee on your first payment. Cancel any time — no lock-in on monthly plans. Annual plans include a 30-day refund window. We're confident you'll stay because of results, not contracts.
How does the performance pricing work? ▾
Enterprise plans can opt for performance pricing: lower base fee ($5,000/mo) plus $350 per qualified meeting booked or 7.5% of closed revenue attributed to Soldiro sequences. Contact sales to model which is best for your deal size and volume.
What integrations do you support? ▾
Native integrations: Clay, Apollo, Hunter, Instantly, Airtable, Cal.com, Slack, and Gmail/Outlook. CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive) on Pro and above. Custom API and webhook integrations on Enterprise. Zapier connect available on all plans.
Do you offer nonprofit or startup discounts? ▾
Yes. Verified nonprofits get 30% off. Early-stage startups (pre-Series A, <20 employees) get 20% off the first 12 months. Educational institutions get custom pricing. Contact sales with verification.
