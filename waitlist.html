The first 50 founding partners get access in 2 weeks (Q2 2026 soft launch). Full public launch follows in Q3. Founding partners join our private Slack and co-shape the roadmap with our team before general availability.
What is founding partner pricing? ▾
Core infrastructure starts at $2,500/mo and Pro at $5,000/mo. Founding partners (first 50) lock in 20% off for life — $2,000/mo and $4,000/mo. This discount never expires. Founding partners also have access to performance-aligned pricing options at launch.
What does deployment actually involve? ▾
Zero technical lift from your team. We configure all integrations, map your ICP, set up your campaign infrastructure, and train your team. Founding partners receive white-glove onboarding with a dedicated implementation manager. Most go live within 5 business days.
What is performance-aligned pricing? ▾
Founding partners can elect to move a portion of their monthly fees to outcome-based pricing at launch — tied to revenue generated, qualified pipeline created, or meetings booked. This model aligns our incentives completely with your revenue outcomes. Details are finalized during onboarding.
Which integrations are live at launch? ▾
Clay, Apollo, Hunter, Instantly, Airtable, Cal.com, Gmail, Outlook, Slack, and HubSpot at launch. Salesforce, Pipedrive, and custom CRM connections follow in Q3 2026. Founding partners vote on integration priority.
Is there a guarantee? ▾
30-day money-back guarantee on your first payment. No conditions, no questions. We are confident you'll see qualified pipeline within the first week. Most partners book their first buyer meeting within 48 hours of going live.
